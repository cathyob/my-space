About Cathy O'Brien

My name is Cathy and I am a transplant from Texas that moved to Boston on a whim in 2015 for change and adventure! I've set down roots, cheer for (almost) all the Boston teams, and am part of the leadership teams for the Boston chapters of Women Who Code and Tech Ladies.

I picked up HTML and CSS in high school and used it as a hobby for 3-4 years. After 7 years in the insurance industry I decided to kickstart a career in web development so I enrolled in a web developer boot camp. Following 'graduation' in April 2017, I began working as a front end engineer with a digital marketing company.

Web development gives me the chance to do something creative and fulfilling, and provides the opportunity for continual growth and creativity that I was looking for in my career.

Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions, want to work together or discuss an available opportunity, or just to say "hi"!
